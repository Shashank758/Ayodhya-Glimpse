Local Traditions of Ayodhya

Diwali Festival

Diwali, the Festival of Lights, is celebrated with fervor in Ayodhya, the birthplace of Lord Rama. It involves lighting oil lamps, bursting fireworks, exchanging gifts, and enjoying festive meals.
